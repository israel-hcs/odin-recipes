Sunomono

Description

Sunomono is a delicious and simple Japanese cucumber salad made with thinly sliced cucumbers in a sweet and sour vinegar dressing. It makes a refreshing salad or light side dish with salmon or pork

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers, peeled
  • ⅓ cup rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out any large seeds. Slice crosswise into very thin slices.
  3. Place vinegar, sugar, ginger, and salt in a small bowl; mix well to combine. Add cucumber slices and stir to coat.
